About BLAST

BLAST stands for Building Lives Around Sound Truth. We are a youth-centered Christian community focused on spiritual growth, mentorship, fellowship, and purposeful living.

Our aim is to help young people know God deeply, build meaningful relationships, and grow strong in faith and character.

Our Programs

Bible Study

We help young people grow in faith through regular Bible study and discussion.

Mentorship

We support youth with guidance, encouragement, and spiritual mentorship.

Fellowship
