BLAST

Founded in 2023

BLAST is a non-profit organization that empowers youth in building their relationships from scratch to serve the Lord.

We are committed to providing resources, mentorship, and a supportive community to help young individuals grow in their faith and develop meaningful connections with others. Through various programs and initiatives, we aim to inspire and equip the next generation to live purposeful lives rooted in sound truth.

"The unexamined life is not worth living."

About BLAST

BLAST stands for Building Lives Around Sound Truth. We are a youth-centered Christian Community commited to spiritual growth, mentorship, fellowship, and purposeful living.

Our aim is to help young people know God deeply, build meaningful relationships, and become strong in faith and character.

We meet every Saturday for fellowship and Bible study.

Our programs

Bible Study

We help young people grow in faith through regular Bible study and discussions.

Mentorship

We support youth with guidance, encouragement, and spiritual mentorship.

Fellowship

We create a strong Christian Community through meetings, prayer, and connection.
