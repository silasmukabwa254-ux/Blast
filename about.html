Our Story

BLAST stands for Building Lives Around Sound Truth. We began in 2023 with a simple goal: create a community where young people can grow spiritually, build lasting friendships, and find guidance that points them to Christ.

Our Mission

We provide mentorship, resources, and a supportive space that helps young people grow in faith, build character, and live with purpose.

Our Vision

We want every person who joins BLAST to feel seen, encouraged, and equipped to walk in sound truth and become a positive influence in their community.

What guides us

Faith

We keep Christ at the center of everything we do.

Mentorship

We create space for guidance, accountability, and growth.

Fellowship

We build real relationships through prayer, learning, and community.

Service

We encourage youth to use their gifts to serve others well.
